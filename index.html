System Requirements

Supported Devices

YWDP is designed for Android 4.2-4.4 devices with the following requirements:

  • Screen Resolution: 480x800 (WVGA) minimum
  • Android Version: 4.2 (API 17) to 4.4 (API 19)
  • Storage: External SD card recommended for theme and music storage

Note: While the app requires a 480x800 screen resolution, it provides a 4:3 iPod Classic aspect ratio display area with virtual scrollwheel controls, making it compatible with various Android 4.2-4.4 devices including Huawei Ascend Y300, Galaxy S3 Mini, and similar devices.

Help Keep MP3 Players (and Old Phones) Alive

Rockbox is a community-driven open-source project that breathes new life into digital audio players of all shapes, sizes, and ages. By supporting Rockbox development, you're helping preserve and enhance these devices for future generations.

Support the Original Rockbox Project

Rockbox.org is the main project that provides ongoing support and improvements to MP3 players across dozens of manufacturers and models, from classic iPods to modern high-resolution audio players.

  • Code Contributions: Submit patches, bug fixes, and new features via Gerrit Code Review (see Development Guide)
  • Non-Programming Help: Run a BuildClient, help with documentation, test plugins, or assist in the bug tracker
  • Plugins & Codecs: Write new plugins in C or Lua, optimize existing codecs, or contribute new audio codec support
  • New Ports: Help port Rockbox to new devices or improve existing ports

Note: Rockbox does not accept financial donations, but welcomes code contributions, testing, documentation, and other forms of support.

Revive Your Old Android Phone

Have an old Android 4.2-4.4 phone sitting in a drawer? Don't let it go to waste! Install YWDP and transform it into a dedicated portable audio player with the full power of Rockbox.

Benefits of Repurposing Old Phones:

  • Reduce Electronic Waste: Give your old device a new purpose instead of discarding it
  • Dedicated Music Player: Create a distraction-free audio experience without phone calls or notifications
  • Extended Battery Life: Without cellular radios and background apps, battery life improves significantly
  • Cost-Effective: No need to purchase a new dedicated MP3 player
  • Full Rockbox Features: Access to 600+ themes, advanced audio settings, and extensive codec support

Rockbox Across Platforms

Rockbox's open-source architecture has enabled ports to a wide variety of platforms beyond traditional MP3 players, demonstrating its versatility and the dedication of its community.

Traditional MP3 Players

Support for Apple iPods (1st-6th gen), SanDisk Sansa series, iRiver devices, Samsung YP players, FiiO players, and many more. See the complete list of supported devices.

Android Devices

Through ports like Rockbox-Y1 and YWDP, Rockbox runs on Android 4.2-4.4 devices, bringing the full Rockbox experience to smartphones and tablets.

Retrogaming Handhelds

Rockbox has been ported to various retrogaming handheld devices, providing high-quality audio playback alongside gaming capabilities. These ports showcase Rockbox's adaptability to diverse hardware platforms.

Unexpected Platforms

The Rockbox community has explored ports to platforms like the Nintendo 3DS and other devices, demonstrating the project's commitment to supporting hardware of all types. For the latest port information, visit rockbox.org.
